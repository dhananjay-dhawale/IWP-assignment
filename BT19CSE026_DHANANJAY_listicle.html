Laboratories to visit in VNIT Nagpur

Dhananjay

September 2021

1.

Computer Science Laboratory

Location CSE dept

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering, established in 1987, offers B.Tech., M. Tech. and Ph.D. degrees. The department has well qualified and motivated faculty members and support staff. The laboratories are adequately equipped with state-of-the-art facilities. The department has good laboratory facilities with adequate number of desktops. The laboratories are open round the clock and are operated and managed by the students themselves. The students also get access to laser and ink-jet printers. For research work, the department regularly procures specialized hardware and software packages as required for the specific research project. The institute has a very good networking facility.

Recommendation : Different types of servers like those by IBM, Apple, HP, Dell, etc. are installed in the laboratories.

2.

Mechanical Laboratory

Location MECHANICAL dept

Department of Mechanical Engineering is the oldest department started from the inception of the institute (1960). Over the last Six decades, we have grown our expertise and competence in the core Mechanical Engineering curriculum and research. Department has shown significant contribution in research in the domains like bio-medical engineering, CFD, robotics, solar energy and advanced machining.

Recommendation : Innovated mechanical workshop has been established in the premises having many modern machineries.

3.

Chemical Laboratory

Location CHEMICAL dept

"Chemical engineers combine mathematics, chemistry, physics, biology, and economics to tackle challenging fundamental and large-scale industrial problems by developing theories, advanced simulations, and experiments" - HOD chemical dept

Recommendation : Advanced chemical laboratories have been established for UG as well as PG
